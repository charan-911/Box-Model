Some Facts on the Honey Badger

• A fully grown Honey Badger can measure upto a meter long(including the tail) and weigh upto 15 kg.
• Males are larger than Females.
• They don’t see very well but have a powerful sense of smell.
• Infact, a Badger’s sense of smell is 100x better than ours.
• Honey Badger’s skin is too tough and thick like military armor.
• Their skin is also loosely connected to muscle making it almost impossible for a predator to get a grip.
• Bee stings, porcupine quills, and animal bites rarely penetrate their skin.
• They have short thick legs and muscular shoulders.
• The feet are armed with very strong claws measuring upto 1.5 inch.
• They have strong sharp teeth and have jaw pressure powerful enough to crack open a small tortoise.
• They also have window like flaps over ears that close during an attack.
